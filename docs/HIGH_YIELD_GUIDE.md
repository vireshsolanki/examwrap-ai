# 🎯 User Guide: High-Yield PDF Preparation

To get the most out of **ExamWrapAI**, we recommend using **High-Yield Mode**. Instead of the AI speculating on what might be important, you can directly guide it by highlighting your study material.

### 1. How it Works
Our engine scans your PDF for Adobe-standard **Highlight annotations**. When it finds them, it extracts the exact text and tags it as "High-Yield" (High Priority). 

### 2. Preparation Rules
*   **Use Standard Highlighting**: Use tools like Adobe Acrobat, Preview (Mac), or Chrome PDF viewer to highlight text.
*   **Focus on Definitions**: Highlight key terms, formulas, and critical dates.
*   **Highlight the "Why"**: Highlight the specific reasoning for a fact, as this helps the AI generate difficult "Assertion-Reasoning" questions.
*   **Avoid Over-Highlighting**: If you highlight the whole page, nothing is high-priority! Aim for 10-15% of the text.

### 3. Why use High-Yield?
*   **Zero Hallucination**: The AI is strictly instructed to generate questions *only* from your curated highlights if they are present.
*   ** JEE/NEET Accuracy**: For complex science topics, your curation ensures the AI doesn't miss subtle but important technical points.
*   **Personalization**: You effectively "program" the exam based on what *you* find difficult.

### 4. Step-by-Step
1.  Open your PDF in any reader.
2.  Highlight the sections you want to be tested on.
3.  **Save** the PDF.
4.  Upload to **ExamWrapAI**.
5.  Watch the AI focus its intelligence on your curated content.
